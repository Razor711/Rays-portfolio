Viennese Chicken

This is a two pan meal that everyone loves. Just serve with rice. Kids just love this!

By Unknown Prep: 15 mins Cook: 1 hr Total: 1 hr 15 mins Servings: 6 Yield: 6 servings Crispy Rosemary Chicken and Fries

Ingredients

  • 2-3 lbs chicken breast, boneless and skinless, cut up into bite size pieces
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2 or more carrots, chopped
  • 6 mushrooms, sliced (optional)
  • 1 can of Rotel
  • 1 cup of chicken broth
  • teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 4 servings of rice

Directions

Step 1

Melt butter in a heavy skillet. Add the onion and saute until tender but not browned. Add the chicken pieces and some garlic and seasoning salt and brown on all sides.

Step 2

Add the green pepper, carrots, mushrooms, Rotel, chicken broth, paprika, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Cover and simmer 25 to 35 minutes, until the chicken is tender.

Step 3

Blend the flour with the sour cream and stir into skillet. Reheat while stirring, but do not allow to boil. Simmer until the sauce has thickend.

Step 4

Serve over rice.
