Chipotle Ranch Cheese Balls

Inspired by

This is a cheese balls that has been tested and enjoyed. It is great on crackers, chips, or bread.

By Barefeet in the Kitchen Prep:5 mins Chipotle Ranch Cheese Balls

Ingredients

  • 8 oz cream cheese (1 package)
  • 4 oz of cheddar cheese (about 1 cup)
  • 1 Tablespoon sour cream
  • Ranch dip
  • 1/2 chipotle chili

Directions

Step 1

Set the cream cheese out to soften, it should be room temperature.

Step 2

Finely grate the cheddar c heese.

Step 3

Mince the chipotle chili.

Step 4

Place all ingredients in a bowl and mix with a mixer untill well blended. Put in a container and put the container in the refrigerator.
