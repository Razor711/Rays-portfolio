Cajunized Oriental Pork Chops

This is a two pan meal that everyone loves. Just serve with rice. Kids just love this!

By Unknown Prep: 5 mins Cook: 30 min Total: 35 mins Servings: 6 Yield: 6 servings Cajunized Oriental Pork Chops

Ingredients

  • 6 Pork Chops
  • 1 1/2 cups Dry White Wine
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 3 Tablespoons of Soy Sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Red Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 medium Bell Pepper
  • 1 Garlic Clove, chopped
  • 1 can of Pneapple Chunks (15oz.)

Directions

Step 1

Salt and red pepper the pork chops. Brown them slowly in a skillet.

Step 2

Add wine, bell pepper, onion, and garlic. Cover and simmer 25 to 30 minutes. Remove pork chops, being sure to keep them warm.

Step 3

Add soy sauce and syrup from the pineapple. Stir and simmer until more or less thick.

Step 4

Add the pineapple chunks and bring to a boil. Serve over rice.
