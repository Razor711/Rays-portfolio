Ranch Seasoning

Inspired by

This is a good seasoning for dressings, dips, and cheese balls

By Barefeet in the Kitchen Prep:5 mins Ranch Seasoning Mix

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried chives
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon fine sea salt (be very careful)
  • 1/8 teaspoon finely cracked pepper
  • 1 - 3 teaspoons of freshly squeezed lemon juice or white vinegar to taste (optional)

Directions

Step 1

Mix it up and add it to what ever you need it for.
